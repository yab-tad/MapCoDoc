UntypedStorage(https://docs.pytorch.org/docs/2.9/storage.html#torch.UntypedStorage) will be the only storage class. To access the
UntypedStorage(https://docs.pytorch.org/docs/2.9/storage.html#torch.UntypedStorage) directly, use Tensor.untyped_storage()(https://docs.pytorch.org/docs/2.9/generated/torch.Tensor.untyped_storage.html#torch.Tensor.untyped_storage).
